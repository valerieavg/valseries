About The Show

Siyah Beyaz Aşk

Siyah Beyaz Aşk (English title: Black and White Love) is a Turkish romantic drama television series starring İbrahim Çelikkol, Birce Akalay, Muhammet Uzuner, Arzu Gamze Kılınç, Ece Dizdar and Deniz Celiloğlu. It premiered on Kanal D on October 16, 2017 and concluded on May 28, 2018.

Plot: Asli (Birce Akalay) is a successful doctor who is forced to get married to a criminal Ferhat (Ibrahim Celikkol) in order to save her own life. The lives of Asli and Ferhat intersect out of pure coincidence. While trying to catch a man who betrays.

Or also, the life of Ferhat, a dark man who claims lives for a living, is intertwined with the life of Dr. Asli Çinar, who has devoted her life to healing others. Will he claim her life? Or will she be able to heal him?
